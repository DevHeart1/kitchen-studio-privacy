Kitchen Studio Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Kitchen Studio ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Kitchen Studio mobile application.

By using Kitchen Studio, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Camera Access

Kitchen Studio uses your device camera to enable features such as:

  • Detecting ingredients and cooking stages
  • Assisting with real-time cooking guidance
  • Scanning food items and cooking surfaces

Camera data is processed only to provide these features. We do not store or sell camera images or video unless explicitly required for functionality and user interaction.

2. User Input Data

Kitchen Studio may collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Ingredients you add to your pantry
  • Recipes you save
  • Cooking preferences
  • Dietary preferences

This information helps personalize recipe recommendations and cooking assistance.

3. Device Information

We may automatically collect certain device information such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Diagnostic and performance data

This information helps improve the performance and reliability of the application.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Provide AI-powered cooking assistance
  • Generate recipe suggestions
  • Personalize the cooking experience
  • Improve app performance
  • Diagnose technical issues
  • Enhance product features

AI Processing

Kitchen Studio may use artificial intelligence systems to analyze cooking content and provide guidance. This may include:

  • Processing recipe videos
  • Extracting cooking instructions
  • Generating recipe suggestions
  • Providing voice-based cooking assistance

AI processing is used strictly to power app features and improve the user experience.

Data Sharing

Kitchen Studio does not sell, trade, or rent users' personal information.

Information may be shared only when necessary with trusted service providers that help operate the application, such as:

  • Cloud infrastructure providers
  • AI processing services
  • Analytics tools

These services are required to protect user data and use it only for the intended purpose.

Data Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children's Privacy

Kitchen Studio is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child has provided personal information, we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the application or through updated documentation.
